## Writing Tone

- Confident and direct. No filler phrases like "I am passionate about..." or "I believe..."
- Lead with impact and outcomes, not responsibilities
- Active voice. State things, don't hedge.
- Keep cover letter / short-answer fields under 150 words unless asked for more
- Sound like a senior PM who has shipped things, not a student describing coursework
- Numbers first when available — if you have a metric, lead with it
